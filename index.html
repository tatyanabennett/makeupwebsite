Makeup tips 101
Makeup

Do's:

  1. Always match foundation to your skin tone
  2. Apply foundation before concealer. Concealer can be moved if put on before.
  3. Wash your makeup sponges if you use them. Bacteria loves moist places, and using it over again will cause breakouts.
  4. When doing eyebrows, use a shade that is either slightly darker, or matches the color of your eyebrows.
  5. Buy good makeup brushes, it will benefit more in the long run.

Dont's:

  1. Use concealer as an eye primer, its oily and makeup will slide
  2. Use to much mascara, it will clump and look unattractive
  3. Apply with finger tips
  4. Go over board and do to much with your eyebrows
  5. Sleep in your makeup
